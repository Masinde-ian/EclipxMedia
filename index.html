Discovery & Business Alignment

Before any channel strategy is discussed, we conduct a structured discovery sprint that maps your actual business model — not just your marketing goals. This means understanding unit economics: what is your CAC threshold before the business breaks even? What is the LTV:CAC ratio required to justify paid acquisition at scale? What does your sales cycle look like, and where does it create misalignment with last-click attribution?

Most agencies skip this step and build campaigns against the wrong success metric. We've seen accounts optimising for CPL on a product with a 9-month sales cycle — a structural mismatch that no amount of bidding strategy adjustment will fix. Alignment on business outcomes precedes all tactical execution.

Deliverable
Business model canvas + paid growth threshold model
Duration
5–7 business days with async interviews
Data Required
CRM export, historical ad account access, P&L summary
Output Format
Notion workspace + Loom walkthrough + live Q&A session
02 //

Data Infrastructure Verification

We audit every measurement layer before spending a single dollar. Pixel integrity, server-side event parity, GA4 event schema accuracy, CRM data hygiene, and attribution window alignment are all examined before campaign architecture is finalized.

Our verification process uses a 47-point audit protocol that cross-references platform-reported conversions against CRM closed-won data. Discrepancies above 15% trigger a tracking rebuild before campaigns go live. We have found misattribution rates exceeding 60% in accounts that believed their data was clean. Every budget decision downstream of bad data is a compounded error.

Tools Used
Tag Manager, Segment, Postman API testing, GA4 DebugView
Verification Standard
<10% discrepancy between platform and CRM data
Server-Side Stack
Meta CAPI + Google Enhanced Conversions + custom webhooks
