// Operational FAQs — Onboarding & Engagement
Our standard engagement structure is a rolling 90-day contract with a 30-day written termination notice period. We do not require 6 or 12-month lock-ins because we believe client retention should be earned through performance, not contractual obligation. The 90-day minimum exists because meaningful paid media results require at minimum one full audit, one structural rebuild cycle, and sufficient time to exit the learning phase with statistically significant data. There are no setup fees. Management fees are agreed in the proposal and fixed for the first 90-day term, with any adjustments requiring 30 days written notice.
You own everything. Always. Ad accounts must be created in your Business Manager — we work with admin access granted to our agency account, never accounts created in our infrastructure. All creative assets are transferred to you in full upon delivery — source files, video exports, copy documents. Looker Studio dashboards are built in your Google account. All data pipelines (Supermetrics, BigQuery tables, Segment sources) are provisioned in your accounts. Engagement end means we remove ourselves from access. Your data, your accounts, your assets — unconditionally and without leverage.
Data privacy compliance is built into our tracking architecture by default. All server-side tracking implementations include Consent Mode v2 configuration (Google) and relevant data processing agreements. We do not transmit personally identifiable information to ad platforms without explicit consent signal — server-side implementations use hashed email and phone data in compliance with Meta's data use policy and Google's Customer Match requirements. For EU-domiciled clients, all data processing occurs within EU infrastructure where technically feasible. We are registered as a data processor under GDPR and can provide our Data Processing Agreement at the proposal stage.
Our minimum is KES 1,000,000 per month in liquid, deployable ad spend — budget that exists today and is allocated to paid media, not projected future spend. Below KES 1M/month, the overhead of building and maintaining server-side tracking infrastructure, real-time attribution dashboards, and weekly sprint management cannot be amortised appropriately against the management fee structure required to staff an account correctly. If you're currently at KES 200K–KES 800K/month with a credible plan to reach the threshold within 60–90 days, contact us anyway — we'll have an honest conversation. Accounts clearly below threshold receive a direct explanation and referrals to agencies whose model fits their stage.
We work primarily with B2B SaaS, DTC e-commerce, enterprise e-commerce, financial services (FCA/SEC regulated), professional services, and lead-generation businesses. We do not accept engagements in: gambling or sports betting, crypto and unregulated financial products, adult content, tobacco and vaping, multi-level marketing, or any business whose core product is demonstrably harmful. We also decline to work with businesses where we cannot establish a clean measurement foundation — if your CRM data is fundamentally unreliable or your internal compliance structure prevents proper tracking implementation, we cannot produce results worth charging for.
